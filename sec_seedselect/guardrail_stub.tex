\subsection{Failure Guardrail via Confidence Abstention}
\label{sec:guardrail-abstain}

We introduce a confidence-aware abstention policy: if the proxy margin between top-1 and top-2 candidates is below a threshold, we fallback to the default seed instead of forcing a potentially unreliable selection.

\noindent\textbf{Observation.}
A simple abstention mechanism improves expected CD (+1.72\% vs +1.39\%) while cutting severe degradations ($>5\%$ CD increase) from 12.7\% to 5.0\% on the full-set sweep. With validation-only threshold selection and frozen test application, the selected threshold keeps test severe risk at 5.7\% with +0.55\% improvement, showing partial but not perfect distribution transfer.
