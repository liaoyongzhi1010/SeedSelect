\subsection{Mechanism Modeling and Held-Out $K$ Prediction}
\label{sec:mechanism-model}

We explicitly model SeedSelect's expected gain as
\begin{equation}
\Delta_K = G_K\,R_K,
\end{equation}
where $G_K$ is the oracle gain available at budget $K$ and $R_K$ is the selector's gap-recovery ratio. We fit $G_K$ with a power-law saturation $G_\infty(1-K^{-\gamma})$, and fit $R_K$ as a function of measured pairwise ranking quality $p_K$.
