\subsection{Sensitivity to Refiner and Metric}
\label{sec:sensitivity-refiner-metric}

To address dependency concerns, we evaluate SeedSelect under multiple refiner--metric pairings on GSO-300 ($K{=}4$).
\Cref{tab:refiner_metric_sensitivity} shows that Difix3D+ $\times$ LPIPS remains the strongest setting (+1.39\% CD, 56.7\% pairwise), while alternative discrepancy metrics are weaker: 1-SSIM retains a positive but reduced gain (+0.76\%), and L1 underperforms.
No-refinement alternatives (PSNR, CLIP, DINOv2) remain near or below random-level selection quality.
